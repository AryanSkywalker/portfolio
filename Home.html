Programmer At Heart...

I have a deep passion for programming and I've been coding for 7 years now. When I was but a child, my dad bought me and my brother an iPad (2nd generation). I used to play a lot of games on that thing. It started out as an interest in understanding how video games like God of War, Uncharted, and Angry Birds were created. When we moved to Jaipur, my dad bought a PC and my brother introduced me to HTML for developing websites. After that I was hooked. In my free time, I'd learn HTML tags, CSS rules and slowly I moved on to JavaScript. I developed some websites really just for fun. Then I moved on to real programming and started out with Python. I learned mainly through YouTube and other sites rather than books or teachers. I explored a lot in Python, and developed an understanding of modules like OpenCV2, NumPy, Pandas, and Matplotlib. I got interested in machine learning and then I took a course on Deep Learning from IIT Madras. Since then I've been making 'projects' in Python for fun. That's about it and I wish to learn a lot more down the line. Right now, I've been trying to master programming much closer to low-level using C and C++. My favorite language has to be C++.

Artist For Life...
